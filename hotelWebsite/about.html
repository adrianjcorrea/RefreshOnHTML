What Our Guests Say

This is a WONDERFUL place to stay in Boston. It's an historic landmark, as it is the oldest continually operating hotel in the country. On the Freedom Trail, right next to Beacon Hill and very close to the Boston Common. It's across the street from the school that Samuel Adams, Benjamin Franklin and John Hancock attended.

If you are looking for history, great location in Downtown, this is it. Grand lobby with old world decor and opulence. We opted to upgrade our reservation the day before which gave us a larger king suite. Other reviews talked about the size of regular rooms, but real estate square footage is expensive. If you are looking for a larger room, pay a little more. Other plus, our king size bed was a great fit for us!
