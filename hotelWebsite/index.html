The History Of Our Hotel

Opened in 1855 by Harvey D. Parker and located on School Street near the corner of Tremont, not far from the seat of the Massachusetts state government. The hotel was home to the Saturday Club, which met on the fourth Saturday of every month, except during July, August, and September.
